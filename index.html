PROCEDURE

  • STEP 1

    For both of our products, start by cutting your orange/tangerine in half.

  • STEP 2

    Remove all the orange from the inside leaving the peel alone, let it dry for some minutes before step 3. While waiting it to dry, do a little hole in the bottom of the pot

  • Step 3

    Add neem oil to the pot outside and in the inside. After this start filling it with soil and the candles with wax

BENEFITS OF BUYING ARANCIA

We are reducing the use of plastics, chemicals, and other bad products for our planet. Buying from a small buisness reduces the waste that fabrics produce.

Our objetive is to give a second use to something that for others is trash. Orange peels are one of these among all of others. Orange peel has an important benefit that is helping the soil give extra nutrients and vitamins to the plants that normal pots do not give.

We are right now working on two main products that are an orange pot and a candle made of the peel as well. Do you know how much creating a container of a candle can waste? Glass, which is the most common material in candles containers, produce a lot of waste because of the fuel that is being used to create it. That is why we chose orange peel.
